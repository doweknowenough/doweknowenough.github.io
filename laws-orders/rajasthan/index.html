  3. Change in strategy: Need to add link to legislations rather than text versions. Link could be to gazette, Sansad library, Archive etc or link to act after every amendment
  4. Ordering shall be done by year of passing of the principal act
  5. Rules, Circulars, Notifications are to be collected
  6. Amending legislations shall be listed in decreasing order of coming into force
  7. Any act amending multiple acts shall be placed under heading of all such acts
  8. Repealed acts should also go to "acts" folder with similar scheme of naming folder
  9. What about rules formed by PSUs?
  10. What about Finance Acts?

  12. When finished significantly, develop a dynamic software which shows what the act looked like after this amendment i.e. starting with act passed in its original form, I should be able to see what the act looked like before the penultimate amendment to the act was passed
