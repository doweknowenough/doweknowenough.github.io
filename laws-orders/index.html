Tips

  1. 31st December, 2023 is the cutoff date. Trace back acts from this date.
  2. Patience is important. Tracking such large amount of data is going to take years
  3. Currently at least establish the links to the act and rules
  4. In future, see if it is possible to track complete history of the act i.e. keeping a record what was changed via every amendment
  5. Amending legislations shall be listed in decreasing order of coming into force
  6. Any act amending multiple acts shall be placed under heading of all such acts
  7. Rules, Circulars, Notifications are to be collected
  8. Ordering shall be done by year of passing of the principal act
  9. Separate section in website for acts which are no longer in force
  10. Separate section in website for acts or parts thereof which were passed but not currently in force i.e. stay on implementation or execution or not notifying in the gazette
  11. If possible, make different sections which can then be combined into codes. This listing can be made on the home page of this section only. Eg- Finance section shall incorporate Indian Sharemarket Code which shall incorporate SEBI Act, Securities Act etc.
  12. What about Finance Acts?
  13. Cross check names of acts when working on them and update them in the database
  14. Separate folder for acts will be helpful when repealed acts are added later
  15. When finished significantly, develop a dynamic software which shows what the act looked like after this amendment i.e. starting with act passed in its original form, I should be able to see what the act looked like before the penultimate amendment to the act was passed
  16. What about rules formed by PSUs?
