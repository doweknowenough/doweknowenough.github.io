Tips

  1. Patience is important. Tracking such large amount of data is going to take years.
  2. Currently at least establish the links to the act and rules.
  3. Amending legislations shall be listed in decreasing order of coming into force
  4. Any act amending multiple acts shall be placed under heading of all such acts
  5. Rules, Circulars, Notifications are to be collected
  6. Ordering shall be done by year of passing of the principal act
  7. Separate section in website for acts which are no longer in force
  8. Separate section in website for acts or parts thereof which were passed but not currently in force i.e. stay on implementation or execution or not notifying in the gazette
  9. Constitution shall be placed under a different section in the website
  10. In future, see if it is possible to track complete history of the act i.e. keeping a record what was changed via every amendment
Format shall be as shown below

Act name, Year of enactment

  1. Link to the latest text of the law
  2. List of amendments made to the act
    1. First Amending Act
      1. Section 6, Provision 1, Sub provision 2 as stood before the amendment
      2. Section 7 as stood before the amendment
    2. Second Amending Act
      1. Section 6, Provision 1, Sub provision 2 as stood before the amendment
      2. Section 7 as stood before the amendment
  3. Rules
    1. Name of rules, Year of enactment
      1. Changes brought by the Amending rules, Year
        1. Rule 4, sub rule 3, sub sub rule 5 as stood before the amending rules
    2. Name of rules, Year of enactment
      1. Changes brought by the Amending rules, Year
        1. Rule 4, sub rule 3, sub sub rule 5 as stood before the amending rules
  4. Miscellaneous
    1. Notwithstanding provisions
    2. Overrides some other act
