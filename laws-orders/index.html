Tips

  1. Patience is important. Tracking such large amount of data is going to take years
  2. Currently at least establish the links to the act and rules
  3. In future, see if it is possible to track complete history of the act i.e. keeping a record what was changed via every amendment
  4. Amending legislations shall be listed in decreasing order of coming into force
  5. Any act amending multiple acts shall be placed under heading of all such acts
  6. Rules, Circulars, Notifications are to be collected
  7. Ordering shall be done by year of passing of the principal act
  8. Separate section in website for acts which are no longer in force
  9. Separate section in website for acts or parts thereof which were passed but not currently in force i.e. stay on implementation or execution or not notifying in the gazette
  10. Constitution shall be placed under a different section in the website
  11. To ensure smooth functioning each principal act can be made into a different webpage and only links to those webpages can be maintained on the home page of this section. This should be done only after significantly completing the project
  12. If possible, make different sections which can then be combined into codes. This listing can be made on the home page of this section only. Eg- Finance section shall incorporate Indian Sharemarket Code which shall incorporate SEBI Act, Securities Act etc.
  13. What about Finance Acts?
